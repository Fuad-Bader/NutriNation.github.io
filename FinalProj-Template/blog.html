Nutrition is the study of nutrients in food, how the body uses them, and the relationship between diet, health, and disease. Good nutrition is essential for maintaining a healthy body and preventing chronic diseases
such as heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.

Carbohydrates
Carbohydrates:

These are the primary source of energy for the body. Good sources of carbohydrates include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes.

Proteins
Proteins:

These are the building blocks of the body and are essential for growth and repair. Good sources of protein include lean meats, poultry, fish, beans, and nuts.

Fats
Fats:

Vitamins:
