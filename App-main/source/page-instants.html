Try the Articles

Tap any Icon

Test dummy instant articles. You can add these to any element you want, in this demo we're showing the power and posibilities.

Show articles instantly

Articles that show instantly from left or right, top or bottom, even as a modal. These are great if you connect them to your own AJAX system, to simulate the use of Google and Facebook Instant Articles.

How do I trigger an Instant Article?

You can trigger an instant article from any element, buttons, images, links icons or even menu items. These support any component. Instant articles also support programatic use!
