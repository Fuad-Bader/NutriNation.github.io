Nutrition

Nutrition is the study of nutrients in food, how the body uses them, and the relationship between diet, health, and disease. Good nutrition is essential for maintaining a healthy body and preventing chronic diseases such as heart diseases, diabetes, and cancer.

Carbohydrates
Carbohydrates:

These are the primary source of energy for the body. Good sources of carbohydrates include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes.

Proteins
Proteins:

These are the building blocks of the body and are essential for growth and repair. Good sources of protein include lean meats, poultry, fish, beans, and nuts.

Fats
Fats:

These are important for providing energy, insulating body organs, and regulating body temperature. Good sources of healthy fats include olive oil, avocados, nuts, and seeds.

Vitamins
Vitamins:

These are essential for various bodily functions such as vision, immunity, and cell growth. Good sources of vitamins include fruits, vegetables, and fortified foods.

Minerals
Minerals:

These are essential for maintaining strong bones, regulating fluid balance, and other bodily functions. Good sources of minerals include dairy products, leafy green vegetables, and seafood.
