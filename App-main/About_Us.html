About Us

  • The NutriNation app is one of the unique applications that allows you to track your diet, health and weight management, as it provides you with a large database about food This app has an easy-to-use user interface and includes integration with third-party tools and services to help you succeed with your diet.


  • Key Features:

1. The ability to use AI technology to communicate with Jess, a nutrition expert, to answer any question related to health.

2. Allows you to record data in a food diary to plan and track what you will eat.

3. The possibility of identifying foods and meals from the pictures to facilitate the calculation of your calories.

4. Possibility of tracking weight.

5. Schedules to record your progress.

6. Great recipes and healthy meal ideas.

7. The ability to sync your account online to access your information from anywhere and at any time.

8. Provide a personalized plan to lose weight or build muscle.

9. Table of calories by searching the database of 500 food items.

10. Keep track of what you eat by recording data in a food diary for all meals.

11. Tracks calories, carbohydrates, proteins and fats.
